Frequently Asked Questions

What is the academy? +

The academy offers distinguished educational courses that help you develop your skills.

What are the payment methods? +

We provide multiple payment methods including credit cards and bank transfers.

Is there a certificate after completing the courses? +

Yes, we offer an accredited certificate upon successful completion of the course.

What is the duration of each course? +

The duration of the course varies depending on the subject and content but ranges between 4 to 12 weeks.

Can fees be refunded? +

Yes, fees can be refunded if canceled before the start of the course.

Are the courses available throughout the year? +

Yes, you can enroll in courses at any time throughout the year.

Is technical support available? +

Yes, we provide 24/7 technical support to assist you in case you encounter any issues.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses? +

Yes, some courses require prior knowledge or specific skills. You can find this information in the course description.

Can I get financial support? +

Yes, we offer financial support programs for eligible students. You can apply for financial support through our website.

Can I access the study materials after completing the course? +

Yes, you can access the study materials for a limited period after completing the course.
